The Towers of Hanoi

The Towers is a simple mathematical puzzle, which consists of three rods and a number of disks of different sizes which can slide on any rod, thus forming a conical shape. The rules are simple:

  • Only one disk can be moved at a time.
  • Each move consists of taking the upper disk from one of the rods and putting it on another one
  • No larger disks may be placed on smaller disks.
Use 1, 2, 3 to move the disks. Alternatively, you can click the rod you want to select. Use the ESC key to abort your current turn.
